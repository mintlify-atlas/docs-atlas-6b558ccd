Learn how to place orders in CaféIES, from browsing the menu to receiving your order confirmation.

## Understanding Time Restrictions

Before you start ordering, it's important to understand that CaféIES operates on a time-based system:

<Note>
  **You can only place orders during specific time windows** that correspond to your shift (morning, afternoon, or evening).
</Note>

### When You Can't Order

If you try to access the menu outside your shift's ordering window, you'll see a banner at the top:

> ⏳ **Pedidos no disponibles ahora** (Orders not available now)
>
> **Próxima ventana**: [Next shift] a las [time] (Next window: [shift] at [time])

This tells you when the next ordering window opens. During restricted times:

- You can browse the menu
- You **cannot** add items to your cart
- The "Add to cart" (+) buttons will show an alert if clicked

<Tip>
  The time windows are configured by the cafeteria staff to match break times and ensure orders are prepared fresh during peak hours.
</Tip>

## Browsing the Menu

When you open the app, you'll land on the **"¿Qué quieres hoy?"** (What do you want today?) home screen.

### Search for Items

Use the search bar at the top to quickly find specific items:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the Search Bar">
    Tap on the search field that says "🔍 Buscar bocadillo, bebida..." (Search for sandwich, drink...)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type Your Query">
    Start typing the name of what you're looking for (e.g., "café", "bocadillo", "agua")
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Results">
    The menu will automatically filter to show matching items as you type
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filter by Category

Below the search bar, you'll see category buttons to filter the menu:

- **Todo** (All): Shows all available products
- Category buttons with emojis (e.g., "🥪 Bocadillos", "☕ Bebidas", "🍰 Postres")

Tap any category button to see only items from that category. The selected category will be highlighted.

### Product Information

Each product card displays:

- **Category emoji**: Visual indicator of the product type
- **Product name**: e.g., "Bocadillo de Jamón"
- **Description**: Brief description of the item
- **Price**: Shown in euros (€)
- **Stock status**: A badge showing availability:
  - High stock: Green indicator
  - Low stock: Orange/yellow warning
  - Out of stock: Red indicator

<Note>
  If an item shows low stock, it's first-come, first-served. Complete your order quickly to secure it!
</Note>

## Adding Items to Cart

Once you've found something you want:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the + Button">
    On the product card, tap the orange **"+"** button in the bottom-right corner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Cart Badge">
    You'll see a number appear on the shopping cart icon (🛒) in the top-right corner, showing how many items are in your cart
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue Shopping">
    Add more items to your cart, or proceed to checkout when ready
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can add multiple quantities of the same item. Just tap the + button multiple times, or adjust quantities in your cart.
</Tip>

### Cart Restrictions

Remember:

- You can only add items during ordering time windows
- If you try to add items outside your shift's window, you'll see an alert:
  > "Pedidos no disponibles" (Orders not available) + information about when ordering reopens

## Review Your Cart

Before checking out, review what you're ordering:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Cart">
    Tap the shopping cart icon (🛒) in the top-right corner of the home screen
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Items">
    You'll see the **"Mi carrito"** (My cart) page with all your selected items
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Quantities">
    For each item, you can:
    - Tap **"+"** to increase quantity
    - Tap **"-"** to decrease quantity
    - If you tap **"-"** at quantity 1, the item will be removed from your cart
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cart Summary

The cart page shows:

- **List of items**: Each with name, unit price, and quantity
- **Total items**: Count of all items in your cart
- **Subtotal**: Sum of all items
- **Total**: Final amount you'll pay (displayed prominently in orange)

<Tip>
  If your cart is empty, you'll see a friendly message: "🛒 Tu carrito está vacío" (Your cart is empty) with a prompt to add items from the menu.
</Tip>

## Adding Order Notes

Before checkout, you can add special instructions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Notes Section">
    Scroll down in your cart to find **"Notas para la cocina"** (Notes for the kitchen)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Instructions">
    Tap the text field and type any special requests, such as:
    - "Sin lechuga" (No lettuce)
    - "Extra picante" (Extra spicy)
    - "Sin cebolla" (No onions)
    - "Bien caliente" (Extra hot)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Keep It Concise">
    Be clear and brief. The kitchen staff will read these notes when preparing your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Notes are optional. If you don't have special requests, you can leave this field empty.
</Note>

## Checkout and Payment

When you're ready to complete your order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Total">
    Check the order summary at the bottom of the cart page:
    - Number of items
    - Subtotal
    - **Total amount** in large orange text
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Pay Button">
    Tap the **"Pagar [amount] €"** (Pay [amount] €) button at the bottom
    
    The button shows the exact amount you'll be charged.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Process Payment">
    The system will process your payment. You'll see a loading indicator while this happens.
    
    <Note>
      The app supports multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay. Your saved payment method will be used.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    If payment is successful, you'll be taken to the confirmation screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Payment Errors

If something goes wrong during checkout, you'll see an error message:

> "No se pudo realizar el pedido. Comprueba tu conexión o el horario." (Could not place order. Check your connection or the schedule.)

This might happen if:

- Your internet connection is unstable
- The ordering window closed while you were checking out
- There's a problem with your payment method
- The server is temporarily unavailable

Try again, and if the problem persists, contact cafeteria staff.

## Order Confirmation

After successful payment, you'll see the confirmation screen:

### What You'll See

✓ **"¡Pedido realizado!"** (Order placed!)

**Your order number** is displayed prominently in large orange text. This is important - you'll need this number to pick up your order!

Below the order number, you'll see:
- **Total paid**: The amount charged
- **Instructions**: "Recoge en el mostrador cuando esté listo" (Pick up at the counter when ready)

### Next Actions

From the confirmation screen, you can:

- **"Ver mis pedidos"** (View my orders): Go to the orders page to track your order status
- **"Seguir pidiendo"** (Continue ordering): Return to the menu to place another order

<Tip>
  Save or remember your order number! You'll need it when picking up your order at the cafeteria counter.
</Tip>

## After Placing Your Order

Once your order is confirmed:

1. **Your cart is cleared**: Ready for your next order
2. **Order is sent to the kitchen**: Staff will see your order immediately
3. **You can track progress**: Use the orders page to see when it's ready
4. **Payment is complete**: No need to bring cash or cards to pick up

<Note>
  Your order will go through several stages: Pendiente (Pending) → En Preparación (In preparation) → Listo (Ready) → Entregado (Delivered). Learn more in the [Order Tracking guide](/guides/users/order-tracking).
</Note>

## Tips for a Smooth Ordering Experience

<Tip>
  **Order during off-peak times** if possible. The cafeteria is busiest at the start of break periods.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check stock levels** before adding items. Low-stock items might run out during busy periods.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Complete orders quickly** during your shift's time window. If the window closes before you check out, you'll need to wait for the next one.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Review your order** before paying. Once payment is processed, you cannot cancel or modify the order through the app.
</Tip>
