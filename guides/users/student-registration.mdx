This guide walks you through the student registration process, from downloading the app to logging in after admin approval.

## Download the App

CaféIES is available as a mobile application for your device. Contact your school's administration or IT department for instructions on how to download and install the app.

<Note>
  The app is distributed internally through your school. You may receive an installation link via email or the school portal.
</Note>

## Create Your Account

When you open the app for the first time, you'll see the login screen. Since you don't have an account yet, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration">
    On the login screen, tap the **"Crear cuenta →"** (Create account) button below the login form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in Your Information">
    Complete the registration form with the following information:
    
    - **Full Name**: Enter your complete name as it appears in school records
    - **School Email**: Use your official school email address (e.g., `usuario@ies.edu`)
    - **Password**: Create a secure password with at least 8 characters
    
    <Tip>
      Make sure to use your official school email address. Personal email addresses won't be accepted.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Shift">
    Choose the shift that matches your class schedule:
    
    - **🌅 Mañana** (Morning): For morning classes
    - **🌤️ Tarde** (Afternoon): For afternoon classes
    - **🌙 Noche** (Evening): For evening classes
    
    Your shift determines when you can place orders, as the cafeteria has specific time windows for each shift.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Tap the **"Registrarme →"** (Register) button to submit your registration.
    
    If successful, you'll see a confirmation message: "¡Registro completado! 🎉" (Registration completed!)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Wait for Admin Validation

After submitting your registration, your account enters a validation period:

<Note>
  **Your account is pending validation by the administrator.** This is a security measure to ensure only authorized students can access the system.
</Note>

### What Happens Next?

1. **Admin Review**: A school administrator will review your registration request
2. **Verification**: They'll verify that you're a legitimate student with the provided email address
3. **Approval**: Once verified, the administrator will activate your account
4. **Notification**: You'll be notified when your account is ready to use

<Tip>
  Validation typically takes 1-2 business days. If you haven't received approval after a few days, contact your school's secretariat or cafeteria staff.
</Tip>

### During the Waiting Period

If you try to log in before your account is approved, you'll see this message:

> "Tu cuenta está pendiente de validación por el administrador." (Your account is pending administrator validation.)

This is normal - just wait for the approval notification.

## Login After Approval

Once your account has been approved by an administrator, you can log in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App">
    Launch the CaféIES app on your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Credentials">
    On the login screen, enter:
    - **Email**: Your school email address
    - **Password**: The password you created during registration
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Login">
    Tap the **"Entrar →"** (Enter) button to access your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Ordering">
    You'll be taken to the main menu where you can browse products and start placing orders!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### I Can't Log In

If you're having trouble logging in:

- **Check your email and password**: Make sure you're using the correct credentials
- **Verify account status**: Your account might still be pending validation
- **Account rejected**: If your registration was rejected, you'll need to contact administration
- **Password issues**: If you forgot your password, contact your school's secretariat

### My Registration Was Rejected

If an administrator rejects your registration:

1. Contact the school secretariat to understand why
2. There may be an issue with your email address or student status
3. You may need to provide additional verification
4. Once resolved, you can register again with the correct information

### Need Help?

The login screen displays a helpful message:

> "¿Problemas? Contacta con secretaría" (Problems? Contact the secretariat)

Don't hesitate to reach out to your school's administrative office for assistance with account issues.
