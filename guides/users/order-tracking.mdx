## Finding Your Orders

CaféIES makes it easy to keep track of all your orders in one place.

### Access the Orders Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App">
    Launch CaféIES on your device and log in if needed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Orders">
    The app uses a bottom navigation bar. Tap the **"Pedidos"** (Orders) tab to view your order history
  </Step>

  <Step title="View All Orders">
    You'll see the **"Mis pedidos"** (My orders) page with a list of all your orders, newest first
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Refresh Your Orders

To get the latest status updates:

- **Pull down** on the orders list to refresh
- The list will reload with current status information
- A loading indicator will appear briefly while refreshing

<Tip>
  The orders page automatically loads fresh data when you navigate to it, but you can pull to refresh at any time for the most up-to-date status.
</Tip>

## Understanding the Orders List

Each order card in your list displays:

### Order Information

- **Order Number**: Displayed as "#001", "#002", etc. (three-digit format)
- **Status Badge**: A colored pill showing the current state (e.g., "Pendiente", "Listo")
- **Items Summary**: A brief description of what you ordered
- **Date and Time**: When you placed the order (format: DD/MM/YYYY HH:MM)
- **Total Amount**: How much you paid, shown in bold

### Status Badge Colors

The status badge uses color coding for quick identification:

- **Gray/Neutral**: Pendiente (Pending)
- **Blue/Yellow**: En Preparación (In preparation)
- **Green**: Listo (Ready for pickup)
- **Darker**: Entregado (Delivered/completed)

<Note>
  If you don't see any orders, the page will show: "📋 Sin pedidos aún" (No orders yet) with a message that your orders will appear here once you place them.
</Note>

## Viewing Order Details

For more information about a specific order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap an Order">
    From the orders list, tap on any order card
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Details">
    You'll be taken to the **order detail page** showing:
    - Complete order information
    - Visual status indicator with emoji
    - Progress bar showing order stages
    - Full list of items ordered
    - Quantity and price for each item
    - Total amount paid
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go Back">
    Tap the back arrow (←) button in the top-left to return to the orders list
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Visual Progress Indicator

The detail page includes a progress bar showing four stages:

1. **Recibido** (Received) - Always completed
2. **En prep.** (In preparation) - Filled when kitchen starts working
3. **Listo** (Ready) - Filled when order is ready for pickup
4. **Recogido** (Picked up) - Filled when you collect your order

The stages that have been completed appear in the accent color, while upcoming stages are grayed out.

## Order Status Meanings

Your order goes through several stages from placement to pickup. Here's what each status means:

### 🕐 Pendiente (Pending)

**What it means:**
- Your order has been successfully placed and paid for
- The cafeteria staff has been notified
- Your order is in the queue waiting to be prepared

**What you should do:**
- Wait for the status to change
- No action needed on your part
- The kitchen will start preparing soon

**Typical duration:** A few minutes, depending on how busy the cafeteria is

<Note>
  During peak times (start of break periods), orders may stay in "Pendiente" status slightly longer as the kitchen works through the queue.
</Note>

### 👨‍🍳 En Preparación (In Preparation)

**What it means:**
- The kitchen staff has started working on your order
- Your food is being prepared right now
- This is the active preparation stage

**What you should do:**
- Your order is being made fresh
- Get ready to head to the cafeteria soon
- Keep the app open or check back in a few minutes

**Typical duration:** 5-15 minutes, depending on the complexity of your order

<Tip>
  If you ordered hot items or made-to-order sandwiches, this stage may take longer than simple grab-and-go items.
</Tip>

### ✅ Listo (Ready)

**What it means:**
- Your order is complete and ready for pickup!
- It's waiting for you at the cafeteria counter
- This is when you need to go pick it up

**What you should do:**
1. **Go to the cafeteria immediately**
2. **Bring your device** with the order number or remember your order number
3. **Go to the pickup counter**
4. **Show or tell staff your order number**
5. **Collect your order**

**Important:** Pick up your order promptly! Food is prepared fresh and should be collected while it's at its best quality.

<Note>
  When your order reaches "Listo" status, the app may send you a notification (if notifications are enabled). Make sure to head to the cafeteria as soon as possible.
</Note>

### 🎉 Entregado (Delivered)

**What it means:**
- You have successfully picked up your order
- The cafeteria staff marked it as delivered when you collected it
- The order is now complete

**What you should do:**
- Enjoy your food!
- This is a completed order and requires no further action
- The order will remain in your history for reference

**Duration:** Permanent - this is the final state

### 🚫 Cancelado (Canceled)

**What it means:**
- The order has been canceled
- This can happen if:
  - The cafeteria was unable to fulfill your order (e.g., ran out of ingredients)
  - There was a system error
  - An administrator canceled it for some reason

**What you should do:**
- Contact the cafeteria staff to understand why
- If payment was processed, you should receive a refund
- You can place a new order if desired

<Note>
  Canceled orders are rare. If your order is canceled, staff should contact you or you should see a notification explaining the reason.
</Note>

## Real-Time Updates

The order status updates automatically as the cafeteria staff processes orders:

### How Updates Work

1. **Kitchen staff uses their interface** to update order status as they work
2. **Status changes instantly** in the system
3. **Your app shows the update** the next time you view the orders page
4. **Pull to refresh** to check for the latest status at any time

<Tip>
  While the system updates in real-time, you need to refresh the orders page to see changes. Pull down on the list or navigate away and back to reload.
</Tip>

### Status Change Flow

Typical order progression:

```
Pendiente → En Preparación → Listo → Entregado
```

The entire process usually takes:
- **Fast orders** (drinks, pre-made items): 5-10 minutes
- **Standard orders** (sandwiches, hot items): 10-20 minutes
- **During peak times**: May take longer

## When to Pick Up Your Order

### Watch for "Listo" Status

The most important status to watch for is **"Listo"** (Ready):

✅ **This means your order is ready NOW**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the App">
    When you see "Listo" status with a green indicator
  </Step>

  <Step title="Head to the Cafeteria">
    Go to the pickup counter immediately
  </Step>

  <Step title="Have Your Order Number Ready">
    Know your order number (shown as #001, #002, etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Collect Your Order">
    Tell the staff your number and they'll give you your order
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Items">
    Quickly check that you received everything before leaving
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Pickup Counter Location

The confirmation message reminds you:

> "Recoge en el mostrador cuando esté listo" (Pick up at the counter when ready)

Look for:
- The main cafeteria pickup counter
- Signs or displays showing order numbers
- Staff members managing pickups

<Tip>
  During busy periods, there may be multiple orders ready. Make sure you clearly communicate your order number to avoid mix-ups!
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

### My Order Is Taking Too Long

If your order has been in "En Preparación" for longer than expected:

1. **Check the current time**: Remember that preparation takes 10-20 minutes on average
2. **Consider peak hours**: The cafeteria is busiest at the start of break periods
3. **Pull to refresh**: Make sure you have the latest status
4. **Check in-person**: If it's been over 20 minutes, visit the counter and ask staff about your order number

### Status Not Updating

If the status seems stuck:

- **Pull down to refresh** the orders list
- **Navigate away and back** to the orders page
- **Check internet connection**: Make sure you're online
- **Close and reopen** the app if needed

<Note>
  The app doesn't automatically refresh in the background. You need to actively check the orders page or pull to refresh to see status updates.
</Note>

### I Missed the Pickup Window

If your order has been "Listo" for a while:

1. Go to the cafeteria as soon as possible
2. Provide your order number to staff
3. They may need to reheat your order if it's gone cold
4. In rare cases, if too much time has passed, you may need to place a new order

<Tip>
  Set a reminder to check your order status 10 minutes after placing it, so you don't miss when it's ready!
</Tip>

### Order Issues or Questions

If you have concerns about your order:

- **Go to the counter**: Speak directly with cafeteria staff
- **Bring your order number**: They can look up your order in their system
- **Show the app**: The order detail page has all the information
- **Contact administration**: For payment or account issues

## Order History

All your orders remain in your order history:

- **View past orders**: See what you've ordered before
- **Check spending**: Total amount appears on each order
- **Reorder favorites**: Remember what you liked
- **Track patterns**: See your order history over time

<Tip>
  Your order history is a great way to quickly reorder your favorite items. Note what you liked and add the same items to your cart next time!
</Tip>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Enable notifications** (if available) so you're alerted when your order is ready.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check status regularly** during the first 15 minutes after placing an order.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Plan your pickup** around class schedules - make sure you can get to the cafeteria when your order is ready.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Order ahead** during your break, not at the last minute, to ensure you have time to pick up before class resumes.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Remember your order number** - take a screenshot or write it down if you tend to forget.
</Tip>
